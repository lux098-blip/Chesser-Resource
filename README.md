What Chesser Resource Is
Chesser Resource is a free online study resource site. We curate, organize, and share educational materials across a wide range of subjects so that learners — whether students preparing for standardized exams, university students working through coursework, or readers exploring classic literature — can find what they need without paywalls or clutter.

Our goal is simple: make good learning material easier to discover and easier to use.

Who This Site Is For
Chesser Resource is built for anyone who learns better with organized, accessible material:

High school students preparing for AP exams, the SAT, or end-of-year coursework.
University students working through math, biology, economics, literature, or humanities subjects.
International learners preparing for the TOEFL iBT or improving their academic English.
Self-taught learners working through a subject independently and at their own pace.
Teachers and tutors searching for free reference material to share with students.
General readers who enjoy classic literature, history, philosophy, and the humanities.
You don’t need an account to use the site, and there’s nothing to pay. Everything here is openly available.

Our Approach
We focus on three things:

Clarity. Educational material is only useful if it can be understood. We prefer plain explanations over jargon and structured layouts over walls of text.

Quality over quantity. Rather than flooding the site with low-effort content, we add resources that are genuinely useful — material we’d actually recommend to a friend.

Accessibility. The site is free to use, mobile-friendly, and designed to load quickly so it works for learners on any device or connection.

About This Domain
A note for visitors who arrived here from older links or references:

The domain chesserresources.com.au was previously owned by Chesser Resources Ltd., an Australian mineral exploration company listed on the ASX (ticker: CHZ). In 2022, Chesser Resources Ltd. was acquired by Fortuna Silver Mines, and the original company’s website was eventually retired. The domain was subsequently released and became publicly available for registration.

We acquired the domain through standard registration channels and have rebuilt it from the ground up as an educational resource site. Chesser Resource has no affiliation with, ownership ties to, or endorsement from Chesser Resources Ltd., Fortuna Silver Mines, or any party connected to the original mining business. The current site is operated independently and is unrelated to the mining or mineral exploration industry.

If you are looking for information about the original Chesser Resources Ltd. mining company or its successor operations under Fortuna Silver Mines (including the Diamba Sud gold project in Senegal), please visit the Fortuna Silver Mines website directly. Historical press releases and corporate announcements from the original company can also be found through public archives such as the ASX announcements platform.

We’ve published this notice so that visitors arriving from older articles, directories, or references are not confused by the mismatch between the domain’s past and its current purpose.
